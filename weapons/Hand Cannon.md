# Hand Cannon

**_Tier 4_** _Primary_ _Physical_ _Weapon_

- **Trait:** Finesse
- **Range:** Very Far
- **Damage:** d6+12 phy
- **Burden:** One-Handed

### FEATURE

**_Reloading:_** After you make an attack, roll a d6. On a 1, you must mark a Stress to reload this weapon before you can fire it again.
