# Blunderbuss

**_Tier 2_** _Primary_ _Physical_ _Weapon_

- **Trait:** Finesse
- **Range:** Close
- **Damage:** d8+6 phy
- **Burden:** Two-Handed

### FEATURE

**_Reloading:_** After you make an attack, roll a d6. On a result of 1, you must mark a Stress to reload this weapon before you can fire it again.
