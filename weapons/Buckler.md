# Buckler

**_Tier 3_** _Secondary_ _Physical_ _Weapon_

- **Trait:** Agility
- **Range:** Melee
- **Damage:** d4+4 phy
- **Burden:** One-Handed

### FEATURE

**_Deflecting:_** When you are attacked, you can mark an Armor Slot to gain a bonus to your Evasion equal to your available Armor Score against the attack.
