# MIGHTY LIZARD

_Tier 3_

(Alligator, Crocodile, Gila Monster, etc.)

> **Trait Bonus:** Instinct +2 | **Evasion Bonus:** Evasion +1
> **Attack:** Melee Instinct d10+7 phy | **Advantages:** attack, sneak, track

## FEATURES

**_Physical Defense:_** You gain a +3 bonus to your damage thresholds.

**_Snapping Strike:_** When you succeed on an attack against a target within Melee range, you can **spend a Hope** to clamp that opponent in your jaws, making them temporarily _Restrained_ and _Vulnerable._
