# Book of Exota

**_Level 4_** _Codex Grimoire._ **_Recall Cost_** _3._

_Repudiate:_ You can interrupt a magical effect taking place. Make a reaction roll using your Spellcast trait. Once per rest on a success, the effect stops and any consequences are avoided.

_Create Construct:_ **Spend a Hope** to choose a group of objects around you and create an animated construct from them that obeys basic commands. Make a **Spellcast Roll** to command them to take action. When necessary, they share your Evasion and traits and their attacks deal **2d10+3** physical damage. You can only maintain one construct at a time, and they fall apart when they take any amount of damage.
