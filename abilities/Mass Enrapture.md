# Mass Enrapture

**_Level 8_** _Grace Spell._ **_Recall Cost_** _3._

Make a **Spellcast Roll** against all targets within Far range. Targets you succeed against become temporarily _Enraptured_. While _Enraptured_, a target's attention is fixed on you, narrowing their field of view and drowning out any sound but your voice. **Mark a Stress** to force all _Enraptured_ targets to mark a Stress, ending this spell.
