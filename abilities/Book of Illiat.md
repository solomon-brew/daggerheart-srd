# Book of Illiat

**_Level 1_** _Codex Grimoire._ **_Recall Cost_** _2._

_Slumber:_ Make a **Spellcast Roll** against a target within Very Close range. On a success, they're _Asleep_ until they take damage or the GM spends a Fear on their turn to clear this condition.

_Arcane Barrage:_ Once per rest, **spend any number of Hope** and shoot magical projectiles that strike a target of your choice within Close range. Roll a number of **d6s** equal to the Hope spent and deal that much magic damage to the target.

_Telepathy:_ **Spend a Hope** to open a line of mental communication with one target you can see. This connection lasts until your next rest or you cast Telepathy again.
